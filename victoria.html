Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial in Kolkata is a grand marble monument dedicated to Queen Victoria. It is a popular tourist destination and museum showcasing British-era history.

Fun Facts

  • Built in 1921, made of white marble.
  • Museum with rare British-era artifacts.
  • Beautiful gardens & fountains.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Nearest airport is NSCBI Airport, Kolkata.
  • By Train: Howrah & Sealdah stations.
  • By Road: Easily accessible from Kolkata city.

Local Cuisine

  • Kolkata biryani, kathi rolls.
  • Bengali sweets: rasgulla, mishti doi.
  • Street food: puchka, jhal muri.

Accommodation

  • Luxury: Taj Bengal, ITC Sonar.
  • Mid-range: The Peerless Inn, The Park Kolkata.
  • Budget: Guesthouses & budget hotels.

Nearby Attractions

  • Indian Museum
  • St. Paul's Cathedral
  • Maidan Park
  • Howrah Bridge

Visiting Tips

  • Visit early to avoid crowds.
  • Check museum hours.
  • Photography allowed (restrictions apply).
  • Wear comfortable shoes.

Best Time to Visit:

The best time to visit Victoria Memorial is from November to February when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

For more information, visit the official website of the Victoria Memorial.
